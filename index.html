Bio

I am a machine learning researcher at the Center for Big Data of Tsinghua University. Meanwhile, I am working on machine learning research advised by professor Qiang Liu at UT Austin. My research interests lies in Trustworthy Machine Learning and Reinforcement Learning. Specifically, my recent projects focuses on (i) learning monotonic neural network, (ii) interpretable machine learning, and (iii) off-policy evaluatin in reinforcment learning.

Prior to joining Tsinghua, I pursued advanced study in science, engineering, and economics first at Beihang, MIT, and Harvard.
